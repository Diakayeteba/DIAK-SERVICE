Diakit-service
# Objective

Create a modern professional showcase website for a computer equipment and office supplies store located in Bamako, Mali.

The website represents the store:

DIAK-SERVICE  
Owner: Abdoul-Kadry DIAKITE  
Location: Marché Didida, Bamako – Mali

The store sells computer equipment, office supplies, and workspace equipment at competitive prices.

The goal of the website is to:

- showcase available products
- attract new customers
- present the store professionally
- allow customers to contact the store easily
- increase local visibility

The website must feel modern, professional and trustworthy.

---

# Context

DIAK-SERVICE is a store located at Marché Didida in Bamako.

The business specializes in selling:

- Computers
- Printers
- Computer accessories
- Office supplies
- Stationery
- Office equipment
- Desk organization materials

The store is known for:

- competitive prices
- availability of products
- good customer service
- local reputation

Target audience:

- students
- businesses
- schools
- offices
- entrepreneurs
- individuals

The website should communicate:

- reliability
- accessibility
- affordability
- product availability
